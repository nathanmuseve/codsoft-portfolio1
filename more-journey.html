My Journey

After I sat for Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education, KCSE, in the year 2017 I knew now my dream was coming close to fulfilment. Things went on well from the time results were announced to the application process in 2018.

After succefully applying for my dream course, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, in some of the best universities in Kenya, University of Nairobi(UoN),Jomo Kenyetta University of Science and Technology(JKUAT), among other choices, I sat back to wait for conformation for I was certain that I have made all necessary requirements for placement.

Whatever followed remains a mystery, even to me up to today. I finally landed for a Bachelor of Science In Economics and Statistics degree course at Egerton University
